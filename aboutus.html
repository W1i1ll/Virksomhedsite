Introduction

Union-T is an EU sales office for 15 knitwear and woven production factories in Italy and 7 in Turkey. We focus on natural and sustainable yarns, recycled and certified organics, including cashmere, wool, cotton, mohair, tencel, alpaca, linen, merino, recycled polyester, recycled polyamide, and more.

We specialize in various kinds of knitwear, i.e. flat and circular knit. Our factories produce machine knit (from 1-21 gauge), hand knit, and hand crochet. We produce sweaters, beanies, scarves, t- shirts, sweatshirts, denims, wool coats and jackets, plaids, activewear, lingerie, socks, tote bags etc.We collaborate with strong and passionate, family driven factories in northern Italy – specializing in the above yarns and hand knits. Our factories in Turkey are primarily located in Istanbul and Izmir and are experts in organic cottons and viscose blends.

Our customers are, among others: Saks Potts (DK), ELSK (DK), Han Kjøbenhavn (DK), GANT (SE), Rabens Saloner (DK), Acne Studios (SE), The Product (NO), Pia Tjelta (NO), Skall Studio (DK), Envelope1976 (NO), Design Letters (DK), Proem Parades (DK), Care By Me (DK), Toteme (SE), Our Legacy (SE), Forét (DK), Another Aspect (DK), IBEN (NO), Soulland (DK), We Norwegians (NO), Wood Wood (DK), and One & Other (NO).

If you are interested, we can provide you with both samples, photos, and quality ref. swatches in various yarn counts. Additionally, we can support you with inspirational material of original styles from the different manufacturers. If you have any type of request or are considering production in Italy or Turkey, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
