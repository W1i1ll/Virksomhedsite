Introduction

Union-T is an EU sales office for 15 knitwear and woven production factories in Italy and 7 in Turkey. We focus on natural and sustainable yarns, recycled and certified organics, including cashmere, wool, cotton, mohair, tencel, alpaca, linen, merino, recycled polyester, recycled polyamide, and more.

We specialize in various kinds of knitwear, i.e. flat and circular knit. Our factories produce machine knit (from 1-21 gauge), hand knit, and hand crochet. We produce sweaters, beanies, scarves, t- shirts, sweatshirts, denims, wool coats and jackets, plaids, activewear, lingerie, socks, tote bags etc.
